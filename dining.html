The Three Broomsticks

Experience the magic of Hogwarts with a meal at The Three Broomsticks, serving delicious British fare and butterbeer!

Mel's Diner

Step back in time and enjoy classic American diner fare at Mel's, where you can savor burgers, fries, and milkshakes!

Krusty Burger

Join Homer Simpson and the gang at Krusty Burger for a fun-filled dining experience with burgers and sides from Springfield!

Fast Food Boulevard

Satisfy your craving for fast food with a variety of options from the Fast Food Boulevard, home to iconic eateries from The Simpsons.
